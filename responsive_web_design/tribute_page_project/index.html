Beyoncé Knowles

    

The iconic singer, songwriter, actress, record producer, dancer, and businesswoman who has captivated millions with her powerful voice, electrifying performances, and inspiring messages.

    
           
    
      

The Journey of a Queen: A Timeline of Beyoncé Knowles' Life:

      
        
              
  • 1981 - Born in Houston, Texas, USA
    •           
  • 1990 - Co-founded Destiny's Child, one of the world's best-selling girl groups of all time.
    •           
  • 2002 - Launched her solo career with the release of "Dangerously in Love," winning five Grammy Awards.
    •           
  • 2006 - Starred in the film "Dreamgirls," earning critical acclaim for her acting performance.
    •           
  • 2008 - Married Jay-Z, a music industry icon, and welcomed their first child, Blue Ivy Carter.
    •           
  • 2013 - Delivered a groundbreaking and visually stunning Super Bowl halftime show.
    •           
  • 2016 - Released the visually arresting and critically acclaimed album "Lemonade," exploring themes of race, feminism, and personal empowerment.
    •           
  • 2018 - Headlined Coachella, becoming the first Black woman to do so, and delivered a historic performance celebrating Black culture and excellence.
    •           
  • 2019 - Voiced Nala in Disney's live-action adaptation of "The Lion King" and released "The Lion King: The Gift" album, showcasing African artists and celebrating Black musical heritage.
    •           
  • 2023 - Embarked on the groundbreaking "Renaissance" World Tour, combining music, art, and technology in a multi-sensory experience.
    •
